Charter Terms

All charters depend on weather, water level, guest count, dock access, route feasibility, and final captain approval.

Captain approval

All routes, pickup locations, destination stops, and charter timing are subject to captain approval.

Weather and water conditions

Trips may be adjusted, rescheduled, or declined due to wind, water level, storms, visibility, unsafe dock conditions, or other operational concerns.

Passenger capacity

The site describes private charters for up to 6 guests. Passenger, child, PFD, and boarding rules are confirmed before booking.

Wildlife viewing

Wildlife sightings are never guaranteed. Guests must not feed, approach, call to, crowd, or disturb wildlife.

Dock access and restaurant stops

Pickup points, restaurant stops, and dock-and-dine routes require dock permission, restaurant timing, route feasibility, and captain approval.

Payments, deposits, cancellation, and refunds

Rates, deposits, payment method, cancellation policy, refund policy, and waiver language are provided before a charter is accepted.

Currituck Sound / Private groups

Ulysses can navigate the shallow local waters and drop off or pick up at privately owned or affiliated partner locations.
