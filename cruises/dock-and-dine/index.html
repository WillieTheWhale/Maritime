Dock-and-Dine Restaurant Charters

Make the trip to dinner part of the evening. Dock-and-dine charters are custom private outings for up to 6 guests when restaurant dockage, weather, timing, and routing allow.

Plan a Dock-and-Dine Charter
Coordinated, not improvised

Restaurant trips need confirmation from every side of the dock.

A dock-and-dine charter depends on restaurant reservations, tie-up permission, water level, arrival windows, weather, route timing, and the captain's judgment. The request form captures the plan so Dauntless can review feasibility before confirming.

  • Up to 6 guests aboard Ulysses.
  • Duck, Coinjock, Corolla, and other soundside destinations by confirmation.
  • Restaurant reservation and dock timing are coordinated before confirmation.
