Golden light, low marsh, and private space aboard Ulysses.

Sunrise and sunset charters are shaped around light, water, and wind. The goal is not speed or noise. It is a private passage with open horizons, soundside air, and room to take in the moment.

  • Private charters for up to 6 guests.
  • Sunrise, sunset, or flexible golden-hour timing by review.
