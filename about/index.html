About Dauntless

Dauntless Maritime Services is built around private, captain-led experiences on the Currituck Sound: shallow water, marsh grass, working docks, sunrise and sunset light, and responsible wildlife viewing.

Positioning

A refined soundside charter, not a generic boat ride.

Dauntless Maritime Services LLC operates scenic, expeditionary inland voyages of the main Currituck Sound and nearby tributaries, such as the Northwest River and Tulls Bay, including the Intracoastal Waterway of northeastern North Carolina through Coinjock Bay and Coinjock, and areas in the northern Albemarle Sound, Kitty Hawk Bay, and Roanoke Sound, aboard Ulysses, a shallow-draft cruising trawler built for quiet access, comfort, and soundside discovery.

  • Capable, not flashy.
  • Literary maritime character through Ulysses.
  • Responsible wildlife viewing without overpromises.
  • Small-group comfort for up to 6 guests.
