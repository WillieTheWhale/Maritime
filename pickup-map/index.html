Outer Banks West / Currituck Sound KOA Holiday

Primary requested pickup and departure point near Waterlily and Coinjock on the Currituck Sound side.

Exact pickup location is subject to KOA permission, dock access, water level, and captain approval.

KOA campground website

Map note: This illustration is for planning and inquiry only, not navigation. Exact departure location, route, pickup timing, and destination feasibility are confirmed after captain review.

Official charts: For actual navigation, Dauntless uses NOAA Chart 12207 (Cape Henry to Currituck Beach Light) and NOAA Chart 12204 (Currituck Beach Light to Wimble Shoals), covering the Currituck Sound from the Virginia state line south to Manteo.

  • Outer Banks West / Currituck Sound KOA Holiday: Primary requested pickup and departure point near Waterlily and Coinjock on the Currituck Sound side. Exact pickup location is subject to KOA permission, dock access, water level, and captain approval. KOA campground website: https://koa.com/campgrounds/outer-banks-west/
  • Duck, North Carolina: Soundside resort town and possible pickup, tour zone, or dock-and-dine area. A specific legal dock or tie-up location must be confirmed before trip details are published. Wikipedia: https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Duck,_North_Carolina
  • Grandy, North Carolina: Mainland Currituck service-area marker for guests staying near Grandy or passing through. Exact waterfront pickup point must be confirmed. Wikipedia: https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Grandy,_North_Carolina
  • Manteo, North Carolina: Extended special-request service area, not a standard Currituck Sound pickup. Routing and timing must be validated by the captain before this area is offered. Wikipedia: https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Manteo,_North_Carolina
  • Carova Beach / Northern Currituck Banks: Context area for responsible wildlife viewing from the water. Wild horse sightings are never guaranteed and all viewing is from a respectful distance. Wikipedia: https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Carova_Beach,_North_Carolina
  • Coinjock, North Carolina: Dock-and-dine destination area with restaurant and marina context. Restaurant reservation, dockage, timing, and route must be confirmed before booking. Coinjock Marina website: https://coinjockmarina.com
  • Corolla, North Carolina: Northern Outer Banks destination context for private charters. Exact soundside dock or restaurant access must be verified before publishing a routable stop. Wikipedia: https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Corolla,_North_Carolina
