Privacy Policy

This policy explains how charter inquiry details are used to respond, review routes, and coordinate guests.

Information collected

The inquiry form may collect name, email, phone, preferred cruise type, preferred date and time, guest count, pickup preference, restaurant destination, notes, and consent acknowledgments.

How information is used

Inquiry details are used to respond to charter requests, review route feasibility, coordinate timing, and follow up with guests. No payment information is collected through this static website.

Third-party services

Charter inquiry details are delivered through FormSubmit to Dauntless' designated inquiry inbox. Cal.com and Stripe handle online charter scheduling and payments under their respective privacy terms.

Data deletion requests

Use the business contact channel listed on the site for privacy questions or deletion requests.
